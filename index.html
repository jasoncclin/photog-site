What is a Pencil?

The humble pencil - at its core, it's the newest writing technology on earth.

It's easy to maintain. It doesn't leak or blot. It never skips.

And it's one of the most inexpensive options for writing, drawing, sketching, or composing.

Kids can use pencils for test-taking. Parents can use them for list-making.

At the PENCILS store, we're passionate about our pencils.

PENCILS was founded online in December 2020 by Heesu Woo, a pencil inventor and lifelong pencil lover.

It's our job to make pencils accessible to those who appreciate them for their functionality and beauty as much as we do.

As simple as it may be, the pencil is something which despite advances in technology will never become obsolete.

Whatever you may use them for, we want to make sure that you can have any pencil your heart, hand, or collection desires.
